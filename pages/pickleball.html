Pickleball

Pickleball in Toronto at Vision Badminton

Explore beginner to intermediate pickleball clinics, lesson options, and next steps for joining at Vision.

Dedicated Pickleball Hub

Visit visionpickleball.ca

For full pickleball program details, schedules, and registration visit our dedicated pickleball website.

Format

5-week clinics

Five-week clinic blocks give players a structured way to build skills and confidence.

Length

90 minutes per session

Each session runs for 90 minutes with time for instruction, drills, and guided play.

Skill path

Beginner to intermediate

Players can quickly identify whether the clinic suits their current level.

Beginner

For newer players learning grip, serve, dinks, volleys, movement, and basic rally confidence.

Advanced Beginner

For players ready to add drops, lobs, better control, and more intentional game decisions.

Intermediate

For players building consistency, better doubles awareness, and smarter shot selection under pressure.

Private Support

For players who want faster feedback, targeted correction, or preparation outside the clinic format.
